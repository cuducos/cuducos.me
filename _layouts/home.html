Hello! I'm Eduardo Cuducos

I’m a geek interested in the social, in culture and in politics. As a PhD in sociology (University of Essex, UK) I enjoy reflecting upon common relationships of our daily life, but my passion for technology introduced me to the idea of hacking — and I might have inherited a hands on mood from my BA in Graphic Design (UFSC, Brazil).

I’m a citizen engagement fellow at the World Bank and one of the co-founders of Operação Serenata de Amor. In addition I love side projects such as Expedição Liberdade, Mútuo and InternetSemLimites. I’m also engaged in open source — founding and collaborating to various projects.

I’m based in Washington, DC. Feel free to join me for a cup or a pint if you believe in the power to the people motto : )

Artificial inteligence for social control of public administration

An open project that uses data science - the same technologies used by giants like Google, Facebook and Netflix - for the purpose of monitoring public spending and sharing information in a way accessible to everyone.

What is behind some of the most amazing schools in the world?

It was with this question that was born the project Expedição Liberdade, by Daniel Larusso, Eduardo Cuducos and Mabel Lazzarin. Between October and November 2013, they visited nine spaces and out-of-box education initiatives across Europe.
